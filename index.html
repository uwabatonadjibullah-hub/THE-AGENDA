Event Agenda & Details

Event Details

Date: Thursday, August 28, 2025
Time: 9:00 AM - 15:00 PM
Venue: KSP Rwanda Main Hall

Agenda of the Event

  • 8:30 AM - 8:50 AM

    Arrival and Setup

    Registration desk, checking eligibility to Participate. Welcoming slow music in the Hall.

  • 8:50 AM - 9:15 AM

    PRAYER AND AGENDA PRESENTATION

    Opening Prayer and introduction of the Agenda by the Event Coordinator.

  • 9:00 AM - 9:15 AM

    Opening Remarks

    An opening and welcoming message from the General Director.

  • 9:15 AM - 10:25 AM

    Project defense session [Phase I]

    Each trainee's presentation (max 5 mins) and Q&A (max 3 mins).

  • 10:25 AM - 10:35 AM

    The Music Break

    Music performance by Pianist Bernard.

  • 10:25 AM - 12:00 PM

    Project defense session [Phase II]

    Continuation of trainee presentations and Q&A.

  • 12:00 PM - 12:15 PM

    A brief Speech of a Special Guest

    An empowering and motivating speech for trainees and trainers.

  • 12:15 PM - 13:00 PM

    A potential Break

    Muslims will pray. Panelists will deliberate projects. Audience will take a fresh air.

  • 13:00 PM - 14:00 PM

    Results Presentation & Best Performer Presentation

    The head of the panel will present the best project. Certificates will be handed over to the best performers.

  • 14:00 PM - 14:25 PM

    Current Status Presentation & The Closing Remark

    The Operations Manager will present ongoing programs and the General Director will share a closing remark.

  • 14:25 PM - 14:30 PM

    The Closing Prayer

    One of the participants will deliver the closing prayer.

N.B: The event is expected to close by 15:00 considering the unforeseen circumstances that may show up.
